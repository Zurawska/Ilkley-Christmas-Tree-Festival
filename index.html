Ilkley
Christmas Tree Festival

Christmas 2018 - exact dates to be confirmed

Born out of a love of Christmas, charity, and the Ilkley community... and taking inspiration from Christmas Tree Festivals in towns up and down the UK... a small team of ilkley residents will be bringing the first Christmas Tree Festival to Ilkley in 2018.

Taking place in a central Ilkley location, the Christmas Tree Festival will run over two days in the build up to Christmas 2018.

Local community groups and charities will each decorate a tree (real or fake) in whatever style they wish, resulting in a magical display of over 20 decorated trees lighting up the venue.

Members of the public will donate a fee to the chosen charity, to visit the display, soak up the festive atmosphere and place their vote for the best decorated tree.

Local music groups and choirs will be performing at intervals and festive nibbles and refreshments will be available.

It's going to be a unique event; designed to raise money for local causes, bring the community together and heighten the magic of Christmas.
